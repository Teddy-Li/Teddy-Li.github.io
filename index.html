About Me

I am a 3rd-year PhD student at the University of Edinburgh, supervised by Mark Steedman. My research interests lie in the field of Natural Language Processing, especially in the area of Natural Language Inference. I am also interested in the automated construction of knowledge graphs and their applications in NLP. Check out my publications for more details :)

I am dedicated to the development of open-source resources. I have developed a few open-source projects, including Chinese Fine-grained Entity Typing (Code and Dataset!), Chinese OpenIE, and a Chinese Entailment Graph.
