About Me

I am a 3rd-year PhD student at the University of Edinburgh, supervised by Mark Steedman. My research interests lie in the field of Natural Language Processing, especially in the area of Natural Language Inference. I am also interested in the automated construction of knowledge graphs and their applications in NLP. Check out my publications for more details :)

I am dedicated to the development of open-source software. I have developed a few open-source projects, including Chinese Fine-grained Entity Typing, Chinese OpenIE, and a Chinese entailment graph.

In informal occasions, I like to go by the name Teddy, but do search for TIANYI LI for my publications! Personally, I am a big fan of road trips and cycle touring. Check out the selected images below!
